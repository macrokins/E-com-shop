Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping, but we will ship any replacements at no additional charge.
If a purchased item is still under warranty, please contact the service center with your receipt of purchase or visit one of our retail outlets.

Please note that it may take up to two weeks for products to be repaired and returned.

Refund Requests

Once we have received and inspected your return, we will send you an email to confirm receipt. We will also inform you of whether your refund has been approved or rejected. If approved, your refund will be processed and the credit will be applied to your credit card or original payment method within a certain number of days. Goods that are in 100% good condition will be accepted, but a 15% surcharge will apply. The goods must be returned within 24 hours of purchase.

Exchange Requests

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged.

If you need to exchange the item for the same item, once the returned item is received, a new order will be processed for your exchange and the shipping details will be provided to you.
If the new item is of a higher price, we will contact you for additional payment; if the new item is of a lower price, we will refund the difference to your original form of payment.
If you request an exchange for an out of stock item, we will issue an E-commerce store credit.
